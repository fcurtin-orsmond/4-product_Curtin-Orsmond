History

In 1949, South of the Border Beer Depot was founded by Alan Schafer

In 1954, 20 Hotel rooms were added

The mascot, Pedro was inspired by one of Mr. Schafer's import contacts from Mexico

The 175 Billboards were added soon after, all designed by Mr. Schafer himself
